How to Play

Players should be able to just dive in without a steep learning curve, and it should feel more like a zen moment than a frantic puzzle rush.

    Observe fragmented frames

  • 02

    Identify how visual elements connect

  • 03

  • 04

    Align edges and shapes correctly

  • 05

    Complete the image to unlock the next level
